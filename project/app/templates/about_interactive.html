About the Interactives

Expand Your Universe with ViewSpace Interactives

ViewSpace Interactives allow you to explore objects and materials from different perspectives, and discover how we combine information to better understand the universe. These interactives let you see newly forming stars hidden inside giant clouds of dust and gas, search for pulsars in the remains of exploded stars, witness the effects of galaxy collisions, explore ways of detecting and investigating planets orbiting distant stars, and more.

Image Tours

Image Tours offer a way to learn about the composition, processes, and evolution of different parts of the universe, such as the center of our Milky Way galaxy.

How to Use the Image Sliders

Select the Image Sliders tab within the Interactives Library page to access our interactive sliders. Each topic includes a number of interactives, which are accessible by selecting different parts on the main image. Learn how objects in space are made of and how they change over time.

Interactive Apps

Museums, libraries, and other informal learning venues may want to consider installing our ViewSpace Interactive Apps to set up an exhibit of interactive experiences. These provide intuitive access to the Image Sliders or Image Tours on browsers navigating to other websites.

This video provides a look into the installation, configuration, and features of the ViewSpace Interactive Sliders App. We are upgrading the two- sided installation method on the ViewSpace Interactive Tour App. The Interactives Apps, applications supported by NASA’s Universe of Learning program, provide access to both Interactives via ViewSpace. The apps are available on desktops, Android devices, and iPads.

Visit our Setup Guide for installation instructions.
