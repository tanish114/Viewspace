Using ViewSpace in your Resources

ViewSpace videos and interactives are designed to be used easily for a variety of purposes within the informal learning setting. ViewSpace can be incorporated into exhibits, facilitated activities, educational programming, and staff professional development.

Exhibit Space

  • Use the ViewSpace collection as a stand-alone exhibit.
  • Incorporate astronomy and Earth science interactives.
  • Provide visitors access to full video library.
  • Use ViewSpace in lobbies, children's areas, cafes.
  • Support with banners and labels.

Facilitated Activities

  • Use as interactive engagement resource.
  • Use for guided or independent learning tours.
  • Perfect for live presentations and exploration stations.

Educational Programming

  • Useful for multiple age groups.
  • Provide teachers links for follow-up classroom usage.
  • Allow students to continue learning at home.

Training and Professional Development

  • Ideal for docent, intern, and educator training.
  • Encourage independent learning via resources.
  • Support workshop and staff development needs.
