Accessibility Statement

A Universe for Everyone

The Space Telescope Science Institute is committed to making the world's astronomical information accessible to all.

We approach this goal in many ways across our websites, sharing the latest scientific discoveries, mission updates, and plans for the future with the public and astronomy community through articles, images, videos, interactives, and other resources.

Recognizing that our audience members come with different skills, interests, and background knowledge, we produce a variety of resources to meet people where they are. We recognize that these resources are only as good as an individual’s ability to use and find them, so we place particular emphasis on website accessibility.

Building Multiple Paths

Many traditional web design techniques do not take into account the needs of all users. As such, we are continuously improving the design of this website.

  • Manual evaluation of the website using automated tools
  • Analysis of user feedback
  • Working with outside expertise and accessibility specialists
  • Using principles that enhance clarity and improve experience
